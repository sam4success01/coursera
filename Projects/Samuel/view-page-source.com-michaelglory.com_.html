Initial Brief.

To get a clear picture of what you need, I start every project by receiving a comprehensive brief of what you want. Having this gives me enough context to properly tailor my services to your project goals.

Redefinition.

Once I am clear on your goals, I’ll be able to provide an indicative web design quote along with a range of implementation strategies, optional inclusion costs and a staged rollout plan.

Sitemap & Wireframing.

This is where I hammer down the layout and general functionality of the site with a mapped out relationship between the various pages of the site and content elements. 

Visual Design.

This is the step where the website starts to take shape. I will create a visual layout that offers the best user experience so that the pages are not only easy on the eye but allow the visitor to quickly find what they need.

Testing.

Before the site is launched, I will place it on a production server where only you and anyone you share the link with can view and test the site. This is a vital step as there will inevitably be issues that need to be addressed before the site goes live.

Launch!

After you’ve done a test run of the site, and all identified issues are fixed, I will transfer the final project to your live server. Once done, all you’ll need to do is get that Tweet ready about your shiny new website!
